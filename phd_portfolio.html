Formal Training Overview:

As part of my Master’s and Ph.D. programs in Instructional Design, Development, and Evaluation at Syracuse University, I received rigorous training in instructional systems design, learning science, assessment strategies, and the application of emerging technologies. This foundation enables me to design evidence-based, scalable learning solutions that align with organizational goals and support diverse learner needs.

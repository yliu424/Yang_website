Project Overview

Welcome to the Knowledge Base on Learning Theories and Instructional Design—a dynamic space for exploring how people learn and how to design meaningful, evidence-based learning experiences. As a dedicated Learning Experience Designer and Researcher, I integrate foundational learning theories, instructional design principles, and research-driven strategies to craft engaging and impactful instruction. This interactive resource showcases Behavioral, Cognitive, and Social Learning Theories through infographics, real-world examples, observation protocols, and reflective insights —bridging theory and practice to support more intentional, learner-centered design.

Project Title:

Immersive Technologies for Educators

Categories:

eLearning

Built Year:

2024

Attributes:

Instructional Design (Followed ADDIE model), Storyboard, Content Creation, Technical Development, Visual Illustration (Infographics, Video tutorial), HTML/CSS, Assessment and Evaluation (Qualtrics Survey, Rubric)

Target Audiences:

Professional Development (Pre-and In-Service Teachers)

Description:

Needs Assessment:

Conduct thorough needs assessments to identify knowledge and skill gaps of technology integration within the Teacher Preparation Programs.

Curriculum Development:

Design and develop comprehensive training curricula, including course outlines, modules, lesson plans, presentations, interactive activities, and assessments.

Content Creation and Adaption:

Create engaging and informative training content that is relevant to the needs of the teacher technology integration preparation, including creating Blackboard course modules, Online learning interface with lesson plan example, adapting technical documentation and user guides, and knowledge base articles.

Collaboration:

Partner with Faculty members, Instructor, technical subject matter experts, service owners and other stakeholders to ensure training materials are accurate, up-to-date, and aligned with goals of Undergraduate Teacher Preparation Programs.

Delivery Methods:

Deliver training programs through various modalities, such as instructor-led training (ILT), virtual instructor-led training (VILT), e-learning modules, Hands-on activity, and self-paced online learning module.

Evaluation and Improvement:

Design and develop evaluation survey using Qualtrics.

Continuously evaluate the effectiveness of training module through surveys, assessments, and feedback from participants. Use data-driven insights to improve and update training materials and delivery methods.

Stay Current:

Remain informed about emerging technologies, industry trends, and best practices in instructional design and user support training.
